Frequently Asked Questions

What primary services does Tezex Engineering offer?

We specialize in comprehensive engineering solutions, including civil construction, industrial fabrication, project management, and structural design consultancy. Our focus is on delivering high-quality, reliable, and timely services for both commercial and industrial clients.

Which regions do you currently operate in?

Our main operations are centered in **Ernakulam, Kerala**, but we are equipped and ready to take on projects across the entire state of Kerala and are open to expanding our services to other parts of South India based on project scale and requirements.

What is your process for starting a new project?

Our process begins with an initial **consultation** to understand your needs. This is followed by a **site assessment**, detailed **proposal and quotation**, contract finalization, and then the structured **execution** phase, complete with regular client updates and quality checks.

How do you ensure the quality of your engineering work?

Quality is our top priority. We adhere strictly to national and international engineering standards, use high-grade materials, employ experienced and certified personnel, and implement a rigorous, multi-stage **Quality Assurance (QA) and Quality Control (QC)** program throughout every project.

Can I get a custom quote for a large-scale industrial project?

Absolutely. We encourage potential clients for large-scale or complex industrial projects to contact us directly via email at **tezexeng@gmail.com** or call us. We will schedule a dedicated meeting to discuss the scope and prepare a tailored, comprehensive quotation.
